🧠 How the PIM-AI Connector Works – From Data to Content

The PIM-AI Connector is an AI-powered middleware that processes structured product data, existing descriptions, and product images to generate high-quality, marketing-ready content.

🔧 For Developers: Built on a microservice architecture using Spring Boot and REST APIs, the Connector accepts JSON data and utilizes OpenAI. Optional: Google Vision image analysis.

🛍️ For Users & Clients: Simply submit your product information – the Connector generates optimized descriptions, image suggestions, and content ready for webshops, PIM systems, or catalogs.

📘 Example:
Provide the title "The Lord of the Rings", author "J.R.R. Tolkien", genre "Fantasy", and keywords.
The connector then generates a persuasive product description – complete with target audience alignment and SEO keywords.
